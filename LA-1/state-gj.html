State Profile: Gujarat

1. List of Major Projects

  • GIFT City (Gujarat International Finance Tec-City): India's first operational smart city and international financial services centre.
  • Mumbai-Ahmedabad High-Speed Rail Corridor: The ongoing construction of India's first bullet train project.

2. Ongoing Developments

  • Energy: Significant investments in solar and wind parks (like the Khavda Renewable Energy Park in Kutch).
  • Industrial: Dholera Special Investment Region (SIR) being developed as a major manufacturing and industrial hub.

3. Importance of the State

  • Economic: A powerhouse of manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and diamond processing. Has the longest coastline in India facilitating heavy maritime trade.
  • Strategic: Acts as the western gateway of India with major ports like Kandla and Mundra.

4. Tourist Attractions

  • Statue of Unity (Kevadia)
  • Rann of Kutch (White Desert)
  • Somnath Temple
  • Gir National Park (Home of the Asiatic Lion)
