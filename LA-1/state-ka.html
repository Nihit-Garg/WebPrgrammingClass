State Profile: Karnataka

1. List of Major Projects

  • Bengaluru Suburban Railway Project: An ongoing mass transit system project to reduce city traffic.
  • Upper Krishna Project: Irrigation project across the Krishna River to provide water to drought-prone areas.

2. Ongoing Developments

  • Technology: Continued expansion of Bengaluru as the "Silicon Valley of India" with new tech parks and startup incubators.
  • Infrastructure: Phase 2 and 3 expansions of Namma Metro and the Bengaluru-Mysuru Expressway.

3. Importance of the State

  • Economic: Leads the country in IT exports, biotechnology, aerospace, and electronics manufacturing.
  • Geographical: Features diverse landscapes including the Western Ghats biodiversity hotspot and extensive coastlines.

4. Tourist Attractions

  • Mysore Palace
  • Hampi Ruins (UNESCO World Heritage Site)
  • Coorg (Kodagu) Coffee Plantations
  • Jog Falls
