State Profile: Tamil Nadu

1. List of Major Projects

  • Chennai Metro Rail Phase-II: A massive ongoing expansion to connect the suburbs to the core city.
  • Kudankulam Nuclear Power Plant Units: Continued construction and expansion of nuclear energy capacity.

2. Ongoing Developments

  • Industrial: The Tamil Nadu Defence Industrial Corridor connecting Chennai, Coimbatore, Hosur, Salem, and Tiruchirappalli.
  • Technology: Expanding data center parks and IT corridors around Chennai and Coimbatore.

3. Importance of the State

  • Economic: One of the most industrialized states in India, leading in automobile manufacturing, textiles, and electronics hardware exports.
  • Cultural: Home to ancient Dravidian temples, Bharatanatyam dance, and classical Tamil literature.

4. Tourist Attractions

  • Meenakshi Amman Temple (Madurai)
  • Brihadeeswarar Temple (Thanjavur)
  • Marina Beach (Chennai)
  • Ooty Hill Station
