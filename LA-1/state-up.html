State Profile: Uttar Pradesh

1. List of Major Projects

  • Ganga Expressway: An ongoing 594 km long expressway project designed to connect Meerut with Prayagraj.
  • Noida International Airport: A greenfield international airport currently under construction in Jewar.

2. Ongoing Developments

  • Infrastructure: Rapid expansion of multi-city metro networks in Lucknow, Kanpur, and Agra.
  • Industrial: The UP Defense Industrial Corridor aimed at boosting manufacturing and foreign investment.

3. Importance of the State

  • Political: It is India's most populous state and sends the highest number of representatives to the national parliament.
  • Cultural & historical: A heartland of diverse cultural heritage, mythology, and vast agricultural yields.

4. Tourist Attractions

  • The Taj Mahal (Agra)
  • Kashi Vishwanath Temple (Varanasi)
  • Ram Mandir (Ayodhya)
